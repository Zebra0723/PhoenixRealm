The little things

A few bits we've thought about so the day doesn't get stressful.

Benches across the park

Twenty benches are placed across the park to provide rest stops between attractions and realms.

Bins near food areas

Ten bins are placed near food areas to keep dining spots clean and tidy throughout the day.

First aid in every realm

Three first aid blocks, one in each realm, keep trained help close wherever you are in the park.

Toilets in every realm

Three toilet blocks, one located in each realm, so facilities are always within easy reach.
