The three realms

Three lands, each with its own atmosphere: relaxed family fun, water rides and sea legends, and genuine white-knuckle thrills. Here's what you'll find in each.

Mythos Family Realm

Mythos Family Realm

Bright, welcoming, adventurous

Mythos Family Realm is designed for guests who want adventure without intensity. It focuses on shared experiences, family-friendly attractions and a welcoming mythological atmosphere. Gentle rides, friendly mythical creatures and open play areas make it the natural starting point for younger explorers and the whole family together.

AudienceFamilies and younger visitors
AtmosphereBright, welcoming, adventurous
Family rides Shared experiences Prize games Mythical creatures Play areas for all ages
HydroRealm

HydroRealm

Mysterious, refreshing, aquatic

HydroRealm brings sea legends to life through water attractions and mysterious aquatic environments. It is designed to feel refreshing, immersive and full of discovery. Flowing water rides and splash zones sit alongside kraken-inspired adventures, so families can cool off together while uncovering the myths of the deep.

AudienceWater enthusiasts and families
AtmosphereMysterious, refreshing, aquatic
Water rides Splash zones Kraken-inspired adventures Family water attractions
Adolescent Abyss

Adolescent Abyss

Darker, intense, dramatic

Adolescent Abyss is created for guests seeking the most intense part of Phoenix Realm. It uses darker mythology, dramatic design and high-energy attractions to deliver a thrill-focused experience. High-speed coasters, towering drops and extreme, dark-themed adventures make this the realm thrill-seekers return for.

AudienceThrill-seekers aged 12+
AtmosphereDarker, intense, dramatic
High-speed coasters Thrill rides for ages 12+ Extreme attractions Tower rides Dark-themed adventures
